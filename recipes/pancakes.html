Description:

This So-So Pancake Recipe makes the most delicious soft and tender pancakes that are just
waiting to be topped with syrup and devoured. Using only kitchen staples they're perfect for a weekend breakfast!


Ingredients for your So-So Pancakes

  • 1½ Cups Flour
  • 4 tsp Baking Powder
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • 3 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • ¼ Cups Milk
  • 4 Tbsp Butter + more for cooking
  • ½ tsp Vanilla Extract
