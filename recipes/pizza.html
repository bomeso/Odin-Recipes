Description:

If you're looking for a So-So homemade pizza recipe, you're in okay place.
This So-So recipe is super easy to throw together, it makes frozen pizza look okay!
Learn how to make the most average pizza of your life with just a few ingredients.

Ingredients for your So-So Pizza-Pie!

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 ½ cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Your choice of Veggies/Meats

Steps for the So-So Pizza

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F, Lightly grease a pizza pan.
  2. Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar.
    Mix and let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth.
    You can do this by hand or use a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook to make it easier.
    Let rest for 5 minutes.
  4. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle.
    Transfer to the prepared pizza pan.
  5. Spread crust with sauce and toppings of your choice.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes.
    Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
